National Day Parade (NDP)

Hyping Up the Public as an Event Promoter

Overview of the Project

I served as a public promoter and motivator at the National Day Parade events. This role placed me right on the frontline of community interaction, where keeping spirits high and ensuring crowd flow required proactive energy and excellent service hospitality.

Key Contributions

  • Public Interaction: Welcomed and engaged thousands of visitors of varying ages, keeping crowd morale high with enthusiasm.
  • Adaptability & Stamina: Worked long hours on foot under hot, humid outdoor conditions, resolving visitor inquiries quickly.

Nursing Connection

High Physical Endurance: Standing and walking for long hours during intense event settings reflects the physical demands of standard ward shifts in healthcare environments.

Dynamic Public Engagement: Hyping up and interacting with strangers of different ages and socio-economic backgrounds helped me build approachable, clear, and empathetic communication skills.

Role

Event Promoter / Crowd Engager

Core Skills

Physical Endurance, Public Relations
