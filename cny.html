🧧 Festive Outreach

CNY Celebration Service

Spreading Love and Festive Cheer to Seniors

Overview of the Project

During the Chinese New Year period, I visited an elderly care home to organize interactive, cultural celebrations. Holidays can be lonely for residents without active family members; our group aimed to bring warmth, tradition, and family spirit back into their space.

Key Contributions

  • Cultural Crafts: Guided residents in designing festive CNY handcrafts to decorate their living spaces.
  • Yusheng Setup: Coordinated the preparation of symbolic 'Lo Hei' (yusheng) tossing sessions safely for elderly consumption.

Nursing Connection

Holistic Well-being: Healthcare is not just physical; it is spiritual and emotional. Providing a festive space addresses the psychosocial health of patients, a critical aspect of holistic nursing.

Collaboration & Team Safety: Cooperating with fellow volunteers to safely organize the food preparation and craft tools built critical teamwork skills. This mirrors the cooperative, cross-functional shift dynamics in ward environments.
