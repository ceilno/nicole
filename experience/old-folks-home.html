Elderly Home Visit

Connecting Generations Through Companionship

Spending time with the elderly has been one of my most formative experiences. I spent my time assisting and interacting with elderly residents through activities like coloring, clay modeling, and deep, meaningful conversations to bring warmth and joy to their day.

NURSING VALUES learned

Empathy & Active Listening

Patience & Understanding

During my visits, I spent hours listening to stories of the residents' pasts, their families, and their life journeys. Many of them faced physical limitations, which required me to speak slowly, maintain supportive eye contact, and practice ultimate patience.

This taught me that healthcare isn't just about administering physical support; it is deeply rooted in emotional companionship. As a future nurse, this experience forms the backbone of my bedside manner.
