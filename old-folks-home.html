👵 Community Eldercare

Elderly Home Visit

Bringing Joy Through Art & Companion Therapy

Overview of the Project

I volunteered at an elderly nursing home to engage the senior residents, keeping them socially active and emotionally supported. Transitioning into healthcare starts with valuing our seniors, and this experience gave me direct exposure to nursing environments.

Key Contributions

  • Art Activities: Facilitated therapeutic colouring sessions and clay modelling activities with residents to exercise their fine motor skills.
  • Active Listening: Engaged in comforting conversations with lonely residents, showing them warmth and companionship.

Nursing Connection

Geriatric Empathy & Art Therapy: Art and clay modelling help senior patients keep their cognitive capabilities active. Observing the physical limitations of the elderly during clay play taught me how to assist them with dignity.

Compassionate Bedside Manners: Working with elders, especially those who may have trouble expressing themselves due to age or health conditions, taught me the importance of patient, compassionate bedside communication.

Target Group

Senior Residents

Values Gained

Empathy, Patience, Dignity
